Humanoïdes

This is the level editor for Humanoïdes.

Choose a tile type from the palette at the bottom right of the screen. Click in the canvas to add a tile. Shift-click to remove a tile. You must add at least one human tile, one gift tile and one humanoid tile.

Click on the link below to try your new level. Right-click and choose “Copy link location” to get a link to your level that you can share with your friends.
